How to Apply

✻

We're in this to make things as simple as possible for you. We get it, you're busy! That's why we have a few methods of applying for a mortgage through our SportsMortgage program.

If you haven't already read about us, SportsMortgage is a program run by Tekamar Mortgages. Therefore, when you apply, you application will actually be going to Tekamar. That just means that any application you make on this site is going to be handled by the fine brokers (us) at Tekamar. Phew. With that out of the way, on to ways to apply.


Option 1: Apply online right here.

Directly below this section is our online application form. It should take about 10 minutes to fill out. Once you hit submit, it will be sent to our office. From there we will go through it, do some digging then give you a call or email you. We aim to get back within 24hrs.

Option 2: Apply with our PDF on the go.

We've gone ahead and made a super awesome PDF that lets you take your application on the go. Don't beleive a PDF can be awesome? We don't blame you. Our's is because after you've filled it out - in the 2 minute breaks you have throughout the day - you can just press the submit button on the bottom of it and we get the info. No need to open an email app. Awesome right?

Option 3: Apply over the phone or in person.

Don't feel like doing this online? No worries, give us a call at 250-832-8766   or visit our contact us page and the team will be happy to help you out!
